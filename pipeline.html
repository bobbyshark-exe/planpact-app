The PlanPact Pipeline

From a chaotic idea in a group chat to a confirmed plan everyone can count on. Here's how we streamline the entire process.

Stage 1: The Chaos

It starts in a group chat. Dozens of messages, conflicting schedules, and non-committal "maybes." The host is left stressed, trying to guess who is actually coming.

Stage 2: Create a Pact

Instead of chaos, you create a Pact. In seconds, you set the what, when, and where, and send a single, clear invitation to your friends.

Stage 3: The Commitment

This is the key. Guests give a simple "I'm In" or "Can't Make It." By removing "maybe," we turn vague interest into a concrete commitment.

Stage 4: Certainty & Confidence

The host now has a real-time, accurate headcount. Everyone gets automatic reminders. The plan happens as it was intended, without the stress and guesswork.
