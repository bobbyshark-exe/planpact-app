Frequently Asked Questions

Is PlanPact free to use?

Yes! The core features of PlanPact for creating events and getting commitments are completely free. We plan to introduce optional premium features for power users in the future.

What happens if someone doesn't respond?

Unanswered invitations are clearly marked as 'Pending.' The host can send a friendly nudge with a single tap, but the headcount will only reflect those who have actively committed.

Can I use this for professional events?

While designed for social planning, its simplicity makes it useful for small, informal team meetups or client lunches where you need a quick, firm headcount without the formality of calendar invites.
